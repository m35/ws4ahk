Embedded Windows Scripting (VBScript & JScript) and COM for Autohotkey

Embed VBScript or JScript directly in your Autohotkey programs. No temporary files. Full and easy access to COM.

For more information, and to contact me (erictheturtle), see the official thread in the Autohotkey forums.
